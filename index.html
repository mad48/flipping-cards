Flipping mode:

Cards direction:

Options:
auto - start flip in automatic mode [true | false]
time - time before all cards flip [ms]
shadow - on/off shadow [true | false]
sequent - sequential time before neighboring cards flip [ms]
flow - row or column display direction [row | column]

Features:
Auto stop on mouse over and on deactivated browser tab

Usage with pure JavaScript:


    <link href="css/flipping.css" rel="stylesheet"/>
    <script src="js/flipping.js"/>
    ...
    <div id="flippingcards" class="flipping">

        <div>
            <button>&#9668;</button>
        </div>


        <div class="deck">
            <div>
                <h1>Deep Blue <br>Sea</h1>
                <img src="images/1.jpg"/>
            </div>
            <div><img src="images/4.jpg"/></div>
            <div><img src="images/7.jpg"/></div>
            <div><img src="images/10.jpg"/></div>
            <div><img src="images/13.jpg"/></div>
        </div>

        ...

        <div>
            <button>&#9658;</button>
        </div>

    </div>

        var options = {
            auto: true,
